Post #1: Engineering in a Nutshell

November 23, 2019

Engineering is a field where anything can happen. One moment, you might be designing a new chipset for Intel, and the next you could be working on the team that makes iOS. It's these often on-the-fly changes that make being an engineer interesting. You get to use your brain in ways that you wouldn't have imagined before working in the profession. Every day is a new and exciting opportunity to show the world, and your employer, what you're made of! Today's post will be about engineers and what they do, how they do it, and if engineering is right for you.
